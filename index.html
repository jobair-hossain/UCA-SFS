What this website includes

The structure mirrors the type of scholarship information students expect to find quickly: purpose, eligibility, award details, required materials, deadlines, review steps, common questions, and direct contact information.

A

About

Explains the scholarship mission, goals, intended student population, and how the program aligns with academic success.

R

Requirements

Lists eligibility rules, required documents, academic expectations, and submission standards in a structured format.

P

Program

Highlights funding, mentoring, enrichment activities, professional development, and renewal expectations.

Q

Questions

Provides FAQs and a dedicated contact page so applicants can get help without emailing multiple offices.

Suggested use cases

  • Department-level undergraduate scholarship website
  • Graduate scholarship or fellowship information site
  • Research participation scholarship program
  • Industry-funded student support initiative
  • First-generation, STEM, or cybersecurity scholarship program

Recommended edits before publishing

  • Replace the placeholder title with your scholarship’s official name.
  • Add the correct application deadline and official application link.
  • Update contact details, office location, and responsible personnel.
  • Insert the exact UCA-approved wording and any college-specific branding.
  • Add an official approved logo if you have permission and the correct asset.
