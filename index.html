CyberAI Scholarship for Service

Build a pathway from UCA to government cybersecurity service.

The ARISE CyberAI Scholarship for Service program prepares students in cybersecurity-related computing fields for government careers through integrated coursework, hands-on CyberAI training, faculty mentoring, research, internships, and workforce preparation.

Why ARISE

ARISE is designed for students who want a rigorous, career-focused pathway into cybersecurity service. The program connects academic preparation with applied CyberAI training, competitions, research, internships, and structured placement support.

Scholarship Support

Financial support that enables full participation

The proposed support model includes tuition and fees, a stipend, books and supplies, professional travel, and certification preparation support.

Applied Learning

Coursework plus research and cyber experimentation

Students develop operational skills through CyberAI modules, faculty-mentored projects, cyber range activities, and cybersecurity competitions.

Career Outcomes

Preparation for government cybersecurity roles

The pathway is aligned with internships, the SFS job fair, and placement into qualifying federal, state, local, or tribal cybersecurity positions.

Eligible academic pathways

ARISE is built around UCA programs that support cybersecurity, secure systems, analytics, and AI-driven cyber defense.

  • BS in Computer Science
  • BS in Cybersecurity
  • BS in Data Science
  • BS in Software Engineering
  • MS in Computer Science

What students will do

  • Complete cybersecurity and AI-aligned coursework.
  • Engage in CyberAI modules embedded in existing courses.
  • Participate in faculty-mentored research and applied labs.
  • Prepare for internships, certifications, and government hiring pathways.
  • Transition into qualifying cybersecurity service after graduation.
