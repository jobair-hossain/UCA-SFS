How to Apply

Application guidance for prospective scholars

Prepare a complete, organized, and professional application that demonstrates both academic readiness and commitment to cybersecurity service.

Recommended application materials

  • Completed scholarship application form
  • Academic transcript or degree audit
  • Résumé highlighting technical preparation and related experience
  • Statement of interest explaining cybersecurity goals and public-service motivation
  • Optional supporting materials requested by the program, such as faculty recommendations or interview responses

What reviewers look for

  • Academic performance and relevant coursework
  • Technical preparation in computing and cybersecurity
  • Demonstrated interest in government service
  • Communication skills and professionalism
  • Potential for success in cybersecurity careers

Tips for a strong application

Be specific

Explain clearly why cybersecurity matters to you, what experiences have prepared you, and how the ARISE pathway fits your career direction.

Show readiness

Highlight courses, projects, competitions, internships, labs, certifications, or technical work that demonstrate preparation.

Write professionally

Use clear academic and professional language, organize materials carefully, and proofread before submission.
