How to Apply

Application guidance for prospective ARISE scholars

Prepare a clear, complete, and thoughtful application that demonstrates academic readiness, interest in CyberAI and cybersecurity, and motivation for public-service career pathways.

Notice: The ARISE CyberAI SFS program is pending funding through NSF Program Solicitation NSF 26-503. Content on this site reflects the proposed program as described in the submitted proposal. Final application instructions and deadlines will be confirmed if the program is funded.

Before you apply

Before beginning your application, review the eligibility requirements carefully and make sure the program aligns with your academic preparation, career interests, and willingness to pursue public-sector cybersecurity service after graduation.

Review Eligibility Requirements Explore the Program

Recommended application materials

  • Completed scholarship application form
  • Academic transcript or degree audit
  • Résumé highlighting technical preparation and relevant experience
  • Statement of interest describing academic goals, CyberAI/cybersecurity interests, and motivation for public service
  • Additional supporting materials requested by the program, such as recommendations or interview responses

What reviewers will likely consider

  • Academic preparation and relevant coursework
  • Interest in cybersecurity, AI, and related technical areas
  • Evidence of motivation for government or public-interest service
  • Communication skills, professionalism, and clarity of purpose
  • Potential to succeed in a rigorous CyberAI scholarship pathway

How to build a strong application

Show clear motivation

Explain why cybersecurity, AI, or CyberAI matters to you, what experiences shaped your interest, and how ARISE fits your long-term goals.

Highlight relevant preparation

Include coursework, projects, labs, research, competitions, certifications, internships, or technical experiences that show readiness for the program.

Write professionally

Use clear, concise, and professional language. Organize your materials carefully and proofread before submission.

Application steps

Step 1: Confirm fit

Review the eligibility requirements and program expectations to determine whether ARISE matches your academic background and career interests.

Step 2: Prepare materials

Gather your transcript, résumé, and statement of interest early so you have time to revise and strengthen your application.

Step 3: Ask questions if needed

If you are unsure about eligibility, fit, or preparation, contact the program before submitting.

Step 4: Submit a polished application

Make sure your materials are complete, professional, and aligned with the mission of the ARISE CyberAI SFS pathway.

Need help before you apply?

Review the selection process, read the FAQs, or contact the ARISE team if you need clarification about eligibility, preparation, or application materials.
