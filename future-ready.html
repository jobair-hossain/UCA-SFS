Future Ready Series

A student-centered professional development series designed to help students explore emerging technologies, strengthen career readiness, and prepare for opportunities in cybersecurity, AI, and computing.

Notice: The ARISE CyberAI SFS program is pending funding through NSF Program Solicitation NSF 26-503. Content on this site reflects the proposed program as described in the submitted proposal.

Why Future Ready?

The Future Ready Series helps students connect classroom learning with real career pathways. Through talks, workshops, panels, and professional development activities, students can learn about emerging areas such as cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, software systems, cloud computing, data science, and Quantum Machine Learning.

For ARISE CyberAI SFS students, the series supports broader preparation for internships, research opportunities, professional networking, and public-service cybersecurity careers.

Visit Future Ready Series

What students can gain

Career awareness

Learn about career pathways in cybersecurity, AI, government service, research, and the technology industry.

Professional preparation

Build confidence through résumé guidance, interview readiness, technical talks, and networking opportunities.

Emerging technology exposure

Explore current and future-facing topics such as CyberAI, secure systems, cloud computing, data analytics, and Quantum Machine Learning.

How it connects to ARISE
