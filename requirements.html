Eligibility requirements

Adapt the categories below to match the scholarship’s real criteria. The sample language is intentionally formal and easy to revise.

CategorySample requirement
Enrollment statusApplicant must be admitted to or enrolled at the University of Central Arkansas as a full-time student for the award period.
Academic standingApplicant must be in good academic standing and meet the minimum GPA threshold established by the scholarship committee.
Program fitApplicant must intend to pursue a degree program, concentration, or activity aligned with the scholarship purpose.
Residency / classificationSpecify whether the scholarship is open to Arkansas residents, non-residents, first-year students, transfer students, or graduate students.
Additional expectationsExamples may include leadership, service, research participation, financial need documentation, or departmental involvement.

Required materials

  • Completed scholarship application form
  • Personal statement or essay
  • Current résumé or curriculum vitae
  • Unofficial or official transcript, as required
  • One or more letters of recommendation
  • Any supplemental material required by the program

Submission standards

  • All materials should be submitted by the posted deadline.
  • Documents should be complete, clearly labeled, and professionally formatted.
  • Late or incomplete applications may not be reviewed.
  • Applicants are responsible for verifying that recommendation letters and transcripts are received on time.
