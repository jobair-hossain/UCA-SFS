Program

Understand the award structure, student benefits, and participation expectations.

What the program may offer

The program page is where you explain what recipients gain beyond the dollar amount. For a strong scholarship website, this page should show that the award is part of a meaningful educational experience rather than only a financial transaction.

  • Annual scholarship funding or stipend support
  • Faculty mentoring or program advising
  • Research, internship, or professional development opportunities
  • Cohort activities, workshops, or leadership development
  • Renewal opportunities based on performance standards

Example award language

Selected students receive scholarship support for the academic year together with structured advising, program engagement expectations, and opportunities for academic or professional enrichment. Continued support may depend on maintaining eligibility, timely enrollment, and satisfactory participation in program activities.

Funding

State the award amount, frequency, disbursement model, and whether the scholarship is renewable.

Engagement

Describe any seminars, mentoring, service expectations, or cohort meetings required of scholars.

Renewal

List the GPA, enrollment, reporting, or participation conditions required for continuation.
