Selection and review process

This page should make the review process transparent and understandable. A well-structured process section helps applicants prepare strong materials and reduces repetitive questions to the department.

Step 1: Application submission

Applicants submit the complete application and all required documents by the posted deadline.

Step 2: Eligibility screening

The scholarship office or committee verifies completeness and minimum eligibility requirements.

Step 3: Committee review

Applications are evaluated using the program’s selection criteria, such as academic merit, leadership, need, fit, or stated goals.

Step 4: Final selection

Top-ranked applicants are recommended for award approval in accordance with the scholarship’s governance process.

Step 5: Notification

Applicants receive an email decision and, when applicable, instructions for acceptance and next steps.

Example review criteria

  • Academic preparation and potential for success
  • Quality and completeness of application materials
  • Strength of personal statement
  • Evidence of leadership, service, or engagement
  • Alignment with the scholarship mission
