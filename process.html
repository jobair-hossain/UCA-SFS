Selection and Onboarding

How the ARISE application process works

The selection process is competitive and is designed to identify students who are academically prepared, motivated for cybersecurity careers, and committed to government service.

1

Review eligibility

Confirm that you meet the citizenship, academic, and degree-program expectations and that you understand the service commitment associated with the scholarship.

2

Prepare your materials

Assemble your transcript, résumé, statement of interest, and other required materials. Present your background in a clear and well-organized way.

3

Submit your application

Applications are reviewed competitively by program leadership and faculty reviewers using academic, technical, and service-oriented criteria.

4

Interview and evaluation

Selected applicants may be invited for interviews or additional review steps focused on communication, motivation, and potential for success in the program.

5

Final eligibility verification

Final selection must satisfy SFS-related eligibility expectations, including academic standing and program fit.

6

Onboarding and counseling

Students who are selected will complete required program counseling and begin structured preparation for coursework, internships, mentoring, and future placement.
